For a constant kkk, find the kkk-shortest paths from sss to every vertex vvv in a directed graph.

Let T(m,n)T(m,n)T(m,n) be the running time of Dijkstra’s algorithm on graph of mmm edges and nnn vertices. For k=1,2k=1,2k=1,2, it can be done in O(T(m,n))O(T(m,n))O(T(m,n)) time, see this.
